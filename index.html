This is the unofficial home page of a little LaTeX class (a spin-off, actually) I wrote years ago as a small contribution to the LaTeX Calendar Bundle. What you can find here is always the latest "snapshot"; the real, official release is available on CTAN and is probably included in your TeX/LaTeX distribution, so you really don't need to download it: look at your TeX/LaTeX distribution's package manager.

Contributing
